Features

Search & filter

Find cameras by name, city, country, or tags. Filter by region, category, and stream type.

Favorites

Star feeds you like and browse favorites only. Block bad streams with skip & hide.

Live playback

HLS video on Android. Health badges, fullscreen, and open-in-browser when embeds fail.

Privacy-first

Camera catalog ships with the app. No remote fetch at startup. Public streams only.

Web note: Most traffic cameras use HLS (.m3u8), which Chrome cannot play inside the web app. Use the Android app for the best experience, or open individual streams in your browser.
